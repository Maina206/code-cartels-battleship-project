BattleShip Tutorial

Introduction to Battleship

Battleship is a strategic game where players try to sink each other's ships. The game has a rich history, dating back to the early 20th century...

Step-by-Step Process to Play

Step 1: Players sit opposite each other

Make sure you have a flat surface and enough space for both players.

Step 2: Ensure you have all the ships

Each player should have the following ships: Aircraft Carrier, Battleship, Cruiser, Submarine, and Destroyer.

Step 3: Arrange your ships secretly

Rules of Placement

Ships must be placed either horizontally or vertically, and they cannot overlap.

Visual Representation of How to Arrange the Ships

Step 4: Decide which player goes first

Players can flip a coin or use any other method to determine who goes first.

Step 5: Take turns firing shots

Players take turns announcing a coordinate to fire a shot and see if they hit or miss.

Sink Scenario

Announce when each ship sinks. If every square of a ship gets hit, that ship is considered sunk. Players should inform each other whenever a ship is sunk, naming the type of ship that was hit.
